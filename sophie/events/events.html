day 1

Day one will kick off the festival with a speaker and an activity around consent. This activity will begin around 3 and will be a casual event Golden Gate Park. Participants will be able to meet others as they practice utilizing and teaching consent. Partipcants will also be able to opt into receiving digital files and education materials in order to teach their wider circles this same information. Anyone and everyone is welcome, althouhg the space will prioritize and center survivors.

day 2

Speakers will be staggered throughout the day. Guests are encouraged and welcome to move around the park and take time to reflect, rather than stay seated all day. Day 3 will conclude the festival with 2 craft events. Below is the tentative schedule and more details on each topic. Supplies are provided, but participants are encouraged to bring their own as well!
