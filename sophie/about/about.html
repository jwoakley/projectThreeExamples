about us

We are an organization that seeks to educate young people, specifically those who are high school and college students about consent. We want to cultivate a sex postivite space as often many, due to family, or other factors, are not exposed to this sort of education.

Our organization, Cultivating Consent, feels strongly that the education system has failed young people in its lack of comprehensive sex ed, as well as reactive centered consent education. We want to encourage proactive education, and the normalization of askingf or consent in all situations, not just sexual. We encouarge particpants to share the information and tools they learn with their community. Please feel free to use the email below if you need additional help organizing.

Although the event is free, all donations will go towards bay area groups that promote ant-sexual violence, consent, and sex education. These groups include, SF Women against rape, Bay Area Women against rape and RAINN. Please also consider donating personally and directly to these organizations, or other local groups.
