DAY ONE

Feast your eyes--and your stomach! The first day of Dynasty Festival kicks off
with a fun group of events that celebrate renowned creators and chefs.

TAKASHI MURAKAMI

SCULPTURE GARDEN & ART EXHIBIT

11:00AM - 9:00PM

Takashi Murakami is a Japanese contemporary artist. He works in fine arts media (such as painting and sculpture) as well as commercial media (such as fashion, merchandise, and animation.) He is known for using interesting characteristics of the Japanese artistic tradition.

Murakami will have a series of sculptures and art exhibits on display. Come learn about his creative process and the influence of Japan on his work while enjoying never before seen sculptures!

DON'T YUCK MY YUM!

FOOD TRUCKS & COOKING CLASSES

11:00AM - 9:00PM

Explore a vast variety of traditional dishes from across East Asia! Catered from local Bay Area businesses and chefs, these food trucks will have you salivating.

Resources about the stigma around immigrant foods will also be readily available at our educational kiosk.

DAY TWO

Get inspired by our healing space and guest speaker series of modern-day
pop culture icons as they speak about their crafts and backgrounds.

MICHELLE YEOH

GUEST SPEAKER

11:00AM - 1:00PM

Michelle Yeoh is a world renown Malaysian actress, best known for her roles in "Crazy Rich Asians," "Shang-Chi and the Legend of The Ten Rings," and many Hong Kong films. Now rising to fame with her lead role in the critically acclaimed film, "Everything, Everywhere, All At Once," Yeoh is inspiring a new generation of Asians in the film industry.

Join us as we discuss Yeoh's career, breaking boundaries in both Western and Eastern film industries, and watch a behind-the-scenes video from the set of "Everything, Everywhere, All at Once."

BRETMAN ROCK

GUEST SPEAKER

2:00PM - 4:00PM

Bretman Rock is a Filipinio social media personality from Hawaii. Known for his content surrounding lifestyle, beauty, fitness, and self-care, Bretman has made space for his millions of followers online to be true to themselves.

Come join us as we discuss Bretman's Filipino roots, the intersection of Asian culture with sexuality and gender expression, and his fun upcoming projects! Attendees will also have the opportunity to shop Bretman Rock's collaboration with Crocs.

RINA SAWAYAMA

GUEST SPEAKER

5:00PM - 7:00PM

As a breakthrough Japanese-British pop star, Rina Sawayama has made waves in music, breaking genre definitions and singing about topics such as mental health, generational trauma, and LGBTQ+ love.

Join us for a Q&A with our Day 3 festival headliner as we discuss her groundbreaking success and get an acoustic performance of her greatest hits.

SAFE SANCTUARY

HEALING AND REFLECTION SPACE

11:00AM - 9:00PM

Our healing and reflection space is curated by Asian psychologists and mental health experts aiming to start conversations that center joy, education, and coping with past trauma.

Open all day, come stop by and participate in self-care and educational activities and learn about available mental-health resources and opportunities for Asian creators in the Bay Area and beyond.

SUPPORT AND CONNECT

Dynasty is proud to partner with Cut Fruit Collective for its 2022 festival. Cut Fruit Collective is a grassroots non-profit based in San Francisco/the broader Bay Area that supports AAPI communities through art, publishing, and shared love of food. Together we aim to support our local Asian community in the area while also encouraging people to pursue the arts. A portion of proceeds from tickets and 100% of the proceeds from merchadise sales will go to Cut Fruit Collective's essential work.
