DAY ONE EVENTS
"

Kick-off ceremony

11 AM - 11:30 AM

To kick off the National Creatives' Expo, the president of IATSE, our close event partner, will speak to everyone about the importance of the next three days, including goals that we have accomplished already, goals we are looking to move towards, and recent happenings and announcements for both IATSE and our various creative industries as a whole.

Speed networking

2 PM - 4 PM

Get to know your potential co-workers in the industry! Whether you've been creating for ten years or looking to get a foot in the door, this is the perfect place to meet the people that could help you take your career to the next level. The room will be divided into all different fields, so all are welcome to meet and greet your next potential employer, coworkers, or union partner.

"Why Unions?" Talk

4 PM

CWA District 4 VP Linda Hinton leads a talk about the importance of unions in all of our industries, how unions have directly benefitted so many already, and the ways to get yourself and your co-workers to join or begin the steps of creating a local branch.

DAY TWO EVENTS
"

Unionization Information & Meetup

1 PM - 4 PM

Leaders and workers from both CWA and IATSE will provide direct information about plans for increased unionization and resources available to everyone across all creative fields. Everyone attending is encouraged to spend even just ten minutes exploring the benefits and possibilities of unions.

"What We Deserve and How To Get It"

4 PM - 6 PM

Join an open discussion, lead by industry leaders in animation and game development, about the rights all creative workers deserve in pay, hours, benefits, job security, and more. Take the time to hear from your peers' experiences, and learn what can be done to ensure the fair treatment of all creative workers.

Creatives Night

6 PM - 10 PM

Prepare for a fun night of art, food, and drink at the boathouse. Showcase and explore art from across the industry with free catering and a bar on the side.

DAY THREE EVENTS
"

"How We Can Help Each Other"

1 PM - 3 PM

Learn of the ways that all creative workers can band together to get what is deserved. Talk with industry leaders, learn about their personal experiences in their fields and gain insight into the issues we all face.

Keynote Reveal Event

5 PM - 6:45 PM

You won't want to miss this. Tune in for a special showcase keynote conference revealing never before seen works in video games, movies, animation, and more. New trailers and news for the latest and greatest from all our talented workers, any some exciting surprises.

Closing

7 PM

CWA President Claude Cummings Jr. will close out CWA 2023 with a vision for the future and a big thank you to everyone who attended.
