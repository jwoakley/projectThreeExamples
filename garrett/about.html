ABOUT
The National Creative Expo is our long-running celebration of the hard-workers in the creative industry, mean to unify those in all different fields all around the world for important causes we all face, while appreciating the incredible work done by all. This year, as lay-offs, mistreatment, strikes, and new demands make headlines, our event is as importanta as ever to give all creative workers the voice they deserve and a medium to stand up for themselves.
PARTNERS
Each year we team up with organizations we believe are pushing strongest for the best for you, the workers, and let them help us put on a very special festival.

IATSE, the North American leader in unions in the creative field, is our long-time partner that is vastly important to make the NCE possible each year. IATSE leads unions across the country for animators, writers, actors, artists, and more.

The Communication Workers of America represents more than just creative workers, but in every case is focused on worker's rights, and we are ecstatic to have them on board with our event this year.

The WGA has had a long and eventful year of fighting for workers' rights and we couldn't be happier to have them join us in doing the same at NCE this year.
