About Us

We are a community-driven organization committed to shedding light on the often overlooked and stigmatized topic of mental health within the Asian community.


Our Mission

We want to make it easier to talk about mental health in the Asian community. We believe everyone should have the chance to learn about and understand these issues.


What We Do

We share helpful articles, stories, and advice about ental health. We also have a list of mental health experts who understand our culture. Plus, we host events and support groups.


Our Community

Our community is made up of people who want to make mental health a priority in the Asian community. This includes mental health workers, teachers, and people who have dealt with mental health issues themselves.
