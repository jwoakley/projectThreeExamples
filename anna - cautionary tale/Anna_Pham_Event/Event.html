Our Event
We're excited to have you join our 3-day festival at Golden Gate Park in San Francisco. This event focuses on raising awareness about mental health in the Asian community.

We're excited to have you join our 3-day festival at Golden Gate Park in San Francisco. This event focuses on raising awareness about mental health in the Asian community.

Day 1 - Dec 8th (10AM - 3PM): Opening Ceremony & Panel Discussion

The festival will start with an Asian performance celebrating various cultures. Afterwards, actress Constance Wu will discuss her personal struggles. The day will end with a mental health panel featuring experts Jenny Wang, Michelle Yang, and Pooja Mehta.


Day 2 - Dec 9th (10AM - 3PM): Storytelling, Art Exhibition & Workshops

On the second day, there will be storytelling sessions, an art exhibition on mental health in the Asian community, and workshops on stress management, anxiety, and resilience.


Day 3 - Dec 10th (10AM - 3PM): Yoga, Community Fair & Closing Remarks

The last day begins with a group yoga or meditation session by Ravi Dixit. This will be followed by a community fair with mental health organizations. The event concludes with a summary from the organizers and feedback from attendees.
